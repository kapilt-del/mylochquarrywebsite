Centre information

At lochquarry,enjoy our exciting rope-based activities in a safe and friendly environment. They are led by our trained and experienced professionals. They focus on your safety. Your safety is their priority.

Rope-based activities

All rope-based activities take place on Lochquarry itself and we are proud to say that the full safety equipments are provided.
